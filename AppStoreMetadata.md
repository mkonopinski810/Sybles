## Description

Turn learning into an adventure! Sybles is a quiz game designed for kids ages 6-12 that makes studying math, vocabulary, science, geography, and history genuinely fun.

**Three Ways to Play**
Choose from Classic mode where every correct answer earns coins, Timed mode for a heart-pounding 60-second race, or Streak mode where consecutive correct answers build multiplier chains for massive scores. Each mode rewards a different skill — accuracy, speed, or consistency.

**110+ Questions Across 5 Subjects**
From addition and fractions to state capitals, the solar system, and word definitions, Sybles covers core topics kids encounter in elementary and middle school. Parents and teachers can also create custom question sets tailored to exactly what their child is learning right now.

**A Shop Worth Earning**
Coins earned in-game unlock power-ups like 2x and 5x multipliers, streak shields, time freeze, 50/50 elimination, and double down. Kids learn to strategize — spend now or save for something bigger?

**Collect Them All**
28 collectible animals are waiting to be discovered. Play games to earn creatures ranging from common to legendary rarity. Filling out the collection gives kids a reason to keep coming back and keep learning.

**Express Yourself**
Pick from 9 avatars and 6 visual themes to make the game your own. Unlock new looks as you level up through the XP system.

**Play Together**
Challenge friends on the same WiFi network with local multiplayer, or compete online through Game Center. A parental gate protects online features so grown-ups stay in control.

**Leaderboards & Progress**
Track coins earned, longest streaks, accuracy percentage, and high scores. The XP and leveling system gives kids a clear sense of progress over time.

**Built for Families**
- No ads. Ever.
- No data collection or third-party tracking.
- No in-app purchases.
- Parental gate for online multiplayer.
- All game data stays on the device.
- Native iPad support with sidebar navigation.

Sybles was built by a parent who wanted a screen-time option that actually teaches something. We hope your kids love it.

## App Review Notes

Sybles is a quiz-based learning game for children ages 6-12. It presents multiple-choice questions across five academic subjects (Math, Vocabulary, Science, Geography, History) with a coin-earning and collectible-creature reward loop to keep kids engaged.

**Parental gate for online features:** Online multiplayer via Game Center is protected by a parental gate that requires solving a multiplication problem (e.g., "What is 17 x 4?") that is beyond the target age group's ability to solve quickly. Local multiplayer over the same WiFi network does not require the gate.

**Data handling:** All user data (progress, coins, collections, custom question sets) is stored locally on the device using SwiftData. The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. There are no analytics SDKs, no third-party tracking, and no server-side accounts. Game Center is the only network service used, and only when the parent has approved online play.

**No monetization:** The app contains no ads and no in-app purchases. All content is available at download.

**Custom content:** Parents and teachers can create custom question sets within the app. These are stored locally and are not shared or uploaded anywhere.

To test multiplayer, two devices on the same WiFi network are needed for local mode, or two Game Center accounts for online mode. All other features can be reviewed on a single device.
