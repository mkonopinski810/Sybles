Quiz Game for Kids — Learn, Play & Collect!

Getting Started

Sybles is a quiz game for kids ages 6–12. Choose from three game modes — Classic, Timed, or Streak — and answer questions across Math, Vocabulary, Science, Geography, and History to earn coins, collect animals, and level up.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create custom questions?

Go to the Custom Questions section from the main menu. Tap the + button to add your own questions with four answer choices. Custom question sets are stored on your device and can be used in any game mode.

How does multiplayer work?

Sybles supports two multiplayer modes:

  • Local: Two or more devices on the same Wi-Fi network can play together instantly.
  • Online: Play via Apple Game Center. Online multiplayer is protected by a parental gate to keep kids safe.

What are power-ups?

Power-ups are purchased with coins earned from playing. Options include 2x and 5x score multipliers, streak shields, time freeze, 50/50 elimination, and double down. Use them strategically to maximize your scores.

How do I collect animals?

Animals are earned by playing games. There are 28 collectible animals ranging from common to legendary rarity. Keep playing to discover them all.

Is my data safe?

Yes. All game data is stored locally on your device. Sybles does not collect any personal information, has no ads, no tracking, and no in-app purchases. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

The app isn’t working correctly. What should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, make sure you’re running the latest version from the App Store. If you still need help, contact us below.
